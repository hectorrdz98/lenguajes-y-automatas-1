-- Reserved words --

1.-print
    
    Allow user to print on console the content next to the "print" word,
    if its not a "str" content, it cast it to str and then print it on the console.
    
    Examples:

        print 'Hello world!'
            ~ Hello world!

        print 'Hello ' + 'world!'
            ~ Hellow world!

        print "I'm " + 20 + ' years old'
            ~ I'm 20 years old
